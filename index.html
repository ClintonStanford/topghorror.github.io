TOP G HORROR NIGHT

POWERED BY   

Established in 2016 among the creaking walls of ‘Top G’, Horror Night™ has become a weekly event where obscure and mainstream horror movies alike are enjoyed and appreciated. Re-launched virtually in 2021, films are curated and scored by a panel of expert judges, before a complex algorithm determines their final score. The result is one of the most comprehensive rankings of horror movies of all time, one that continues to grow each and every week. To see the complete list of films, click here.

This site was created in the early days of Horror Night™, and has undergone various changes in that time. Visitors may submit their scores, browse the Hall of Fame, or take a trip down memory lane through the Archives. Check out the Website Development Roadmap for what to expect in future updates.

So sit back, grab your favourite delicacy, and prepare to be spooked by everything Horror Night™ has to offer!
